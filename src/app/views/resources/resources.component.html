Recordings of webinar series 'Bridging GI and Space initiatives fostering gender equality'

This webinar series organized by Women in Copernicus gives an overview of existing initiatives in the Space/EO/GI sector that promote gender equality and at the same time showcases testimonials working in these fields. It is an opportunity to get to know the portfolio and activities carried out by these successful networks and facilitate further networking.
The webinar series took place April-May 2021, every Wednesday at 15:00 (CEST).
