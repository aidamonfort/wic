Women in Copernicus
Core Team

The WiC core team is voluntarily running the network. We want to continue growing the initiative and keep the link between us, despite the ending of the first project.
Meet the ladies behind the WiC Team!

Marie Jagaille

Project manager for regional satellite applications, Brittany Remote Sensing Cluster (BreTel)

I coordinated the first Women in Copernicus survey (2020) and I am now involved in various actions related to the Women in Copernicus initiative: dissemination of results at the European, national and regional levels; implementation of local actions; design of new actions and projects...

Barbara Riedler

RESEACHRCHER AND LECTURER, Z_GIS (University of Salzburg

I am part of the WiC team because it is a bottom-up initiative trying to change reality - where gender equality and inclusion is not achieved yet - in an unique manner full of empathy, respect and inspiration. I support research, outreach - especially in the scientific community, education and networking.

Nathalie Stéphenne

Geomatic expert and coordinator of the Walloon working group on remote sensing in public services, Public Services of Wallonia, Geomatic department

One year ago, I dreamed about this project ad when I talk about it with all these nice partners, the dream came true. I’m engaged and pleased to work for WIC (conference, papers, reports, video, webinars, social media ...)

Grazia Fiore

User Programme Coordinator, EURISY 

I am supporting the WiC team to expand the network and propose subjects of discussions. This amazing project allowed me to meet inspired and inspiring women working in the sector to make inclusion and equality a priority, downstream as well as upstream.

Natassa Antoniou

Project Officer - Market Development, EARSC

WiC has brought me joy, motivation and inspiration during this year of Covid. I am supporting the WiC team to grow, bring more stakeholders in the network and get connected to other exciting/similar initiatives around the world. My motto, “Together we can do great things” and WiC is a great example!

Kathrin Lenvain

Business Development, AZO - Space of Innovation

After a decade working in space, I took it for granted that there are not many women in this sector. WIC changed my mindset on this. I love working in space and want to get others enthusiastic about it too. Only by taking action, there will be change. I support Outreach and Communication Activities and bring in new ideas.
